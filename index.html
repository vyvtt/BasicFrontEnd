Welcom to Our Store

Come into our beautiful flower shop located in the impressive Atrium building in downtown Victoria. Parking is "1st hour free" in all the city parkades. The closest city parkade to Poppies is on Johnson between Douglas and Blanshard.

Our shop offers a large selection of local, seasonal cut flowers, as well as a whole cooler devoted to tropical flowers. We also carry a fabulous selection of tropical plants, orchid plants, air plants, wall planters, and a wonderful assortment of pottery and vases.

We make most of our bouquets and arrangements custom to order, so come in and let one of our experienced designers create something special just for you.

Call us at 250-383-0743 or 1-800-836-9666 and we’ll arrange for quick delivery anywhere in Greater Victoria. You can also order online or contact us by email: flowers@poppiesfloralart.com
